THE MASTER CERAMIST:
JASON J. KIM


Jason J. Kim, CDT is the Founder and President of Jason J. Kim Dental
Aesthetics and owns/operates the Oral Design Center in NYC. He lectures
nationally and internationally and has written numerous articles and
co-authored books on esthetic restoration and team communication,
including “Aesthetic Restorative Dentistry Principles and Practice”.
He is also the sole author of “The Master Ceramist”. Mr. Kim is a member
of the International Oral Design Foundation, Dental Laboratory Association
of New York and the American Society for Dental Aesthetics. He is also
Clinical Assistant Professor at New York University College of Dentistry.

In the hands of a master ceramist, dental restoration done in a lab
is akin to a piece of art. Every detail is taken into consideration,
in order to fulfill each patient’s needs and exceed their expectations.

A Master Ceramist combines technical skill and an aesthetic sensibility
that sets apart the work of a dedicated artist from a tradesman,
and the results exemplify the difference between a mere functional improvement
and life-changing aesthetics.

A PERFECT
PARTNERSHIP


Ensuring there is a balance between dental aesthetics and function is
a collaborative effort between a clinician and a ceramist. Together
they rebuild each patient’s dental structure, additionally this prevents further
deterioration of teeth that occurs with aging. Defining the minimum
amount of tooth reduction needed for an effective restoration is one of
the main goals of this partnership.

Pre-design planning includes: photo reference for color and design analysis,
as well as condyle study for achieving comfortable chewing patterns and
ending with a collaborative diagnosis. This involves determining ceramic color
and tooth positioning, in order to be aesthetically pleasing and comfortable at
the same time. This all occurs before clinical tooth preparation. All of these
steps ensure a minimally invasive tooth reduction for the patient. Breakthrough
smile design with the added benefit of prolonging one’s dental health are
the results of a very successful partnership between Apa Aesthetic Dental &
Consmetic Centre and Jason J. Kim Oral Design New York Center.
